Hypomorphic homozygous mutations in phosphoglucomutase 3 (PGM3) impair immunity and increase serum IgE levels. J Allergy Clin Immunol. 2014 May;133(5):1410-9, 1419.e1-13. doi: 10.1016/j.jaci.2014.02.025. Epub 2014 Apr 1. PMID: 24698316; PMCID: PMC4825677.

Hong Q, Ruhaak LR, Stroble C, Parker E, Huang J, Maverakis E, Lebrilla CB. A Method for Comprehensive Glycosite-Mapping and Direct Quantitation of Serum Glycoproteins. J Proteome Res. 2015 Dec 4;14(12):5179-92. doi: 10.1021/acs.jproteome.5b00756. Epub 2015 Nov 9. PMID: 26510530; PMCID: PMC4670571.

In this example the Glycan format is typical. The tool were able to correctly predict the structure composition; however, because some are presented with very short linkages GlycoCT prediction is inaccurate on some MS table.

This example show potential fault in our current tool which failed to detect correct neighbor if two monosaccharides on different brand are placed too close to one another.
